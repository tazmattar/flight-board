VATSIM Flight Information Display System (FIDS)

Bringing real-world airport board immersion to VATSIM.

Hello fellow aviation geeks,

I built the VATSIM Flight Information Display System (FIDS) to add that extra layer of immersion: the feeling of standing in a terminal at Zurich, Heathrow, or JFK and watching the departures board update in real time.

What started as a personal passion project has grown into a tool used by over a thousand simmers globally. Seeing people run the board on second monitors, iPads, and dedicated Raspberry Pi kiosks has been incredibly rewarding.

I host the flight board myself on a home enterprise-grade server cluster (Proxmox/Ceph). I love keeping it free and open source, but running 24/7 servers and a gigabit fiber connection does add up in electricity and internet costs.

If the flight board adds a little extra joy to your sim experience, a coffee helps keep the servers humming, the lights on, and the flight statuses updating.

Thank you for your support, and see you on the network.
