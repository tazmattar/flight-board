# SimFixr FlightBoard Usage & Branding Policy

## 1. Purpose
SimFixr FlightBoard is a non-commercial project designed exclusively for the flight simulation community (VATSIM). It replicates airport FIDS for immersion purposes only.

## 2. Branding and Logos
All trademarks and logos remain the property of their respective owners.
* **Usage:** Logos are retrieved via third-party APIs or local directories for simulation purposes only.
* **Non-Affiliation:** This project is not affiliated with any airline or airport authority.

## 3. Data & Privacy
To maintain the performance and security of the application, we collect limited usage data:
* **Traffic Analytics:** We record page views, specific paths visited, and airport searches to generate aggregate traffic statistics.
* **Identifiers:** The system uses session-based unique identifiers (UUIDs) and cookies to distinguish between unique visitors and returning users.
* **IP Addresses:** Client IP addresses are processed for security purposes, such as rate-limiting and preventing unauthorized access to administrative functions.
* **Data Retention:** Individual visitor records (first/last seen dates) are stored in a rolling log and are automatically pruned once the database exceeds 20,000 entries.

## 4. Flight Data Disclaimer
* **For Flight Simulation Use Only:** All flight and weather data is sourced from the VATSIM Public Data API. This information is strictly for virtual aviation and must **never** be used for real-world flight planning or navigation.
