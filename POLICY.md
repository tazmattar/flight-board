# SimFixr FlightBoard Usage & Branding Policy

## 1. Purpose
SimFixr FlightBoard is a non-commercial project designed exclusively for the flight simulation community (VATSIM). It replicates airport FIDS for immersion purposes only.

## 2. Branding and Logos
All trademarks and logos remain the property of their respective owners.
* **Usage:** Logos are retrieved via third-party APIs or local directories for simulation purposes only.
* **Non-Affiliation:** This project is not affiliated with any airline or airport authority.

## 3. Data Disclaimer
* **For Flight Simulation Use Only:** Sourced from the VATSIM API, this information is strictly for virtual aviation and must never be used for real-world flight planning.
